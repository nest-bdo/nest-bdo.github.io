About

Relaxed atmosphere, lifeskills and PvE, play your way!

Lifeskills

Are you a master lifeskiller or just starting out? All are welcome! Nest is always seeking more crafters, gatherers, and fishers.

AFK-Friendly

We love our AFK players! Do you spend 99% of your time AFK fishing in Velia while watching Netflix? So do we!

No Requirements

No VOIP, gear score, level, or activity requirements! Play how you want, when you want.

Guild Buffs

Nest currently supports +3 fishing and +2 gathering. We also have a guild galley!
